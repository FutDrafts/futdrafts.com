# Cookie Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how FutDrafts ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, enhancing your browsing experience.

## 3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

- Remember your preferences and settings
- Understand how you use our website
- Improve our services and performance
- Provide personalized content and recommendations
- Ensure the security and proper functioning of our website
- Analyze traffic patterns and user behavior

## 4. Types of Cookies We Use

### 4.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

### 4.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use:

- **Vercel Analytics**: Collects anonymous data about how users interact with our website to help us improve performance and user experience.
- **Vercel Speed Insights**: Monitors and analyzes website performance metrics to optimize loading times and responsiveness.
- **PostHog**: Provides analytics on user behavior and interactions to help us improve our services.

### 4.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

### 4.4 Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and marketing campaigns. They track your browsing habits and help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising.

### 4.5 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties on our behalf:

- **Cloudflare**: Uses cookies (such as `__cf_bm`) to identify trusted web traffic, prevent malicious activity, and support performance and security.

## 5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can:

- Delete all cookies from your browser
- Block all cookies by default
- Allow only first-party cookies
- Browse in private or incognito mode

Please note that restricting cookies may impact your experience on our website and limit certain functionalities.

### Browser-Specific Instructions

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies

## 6. Data Storage and Retention

Cookies can remain on your device for different periods:

- **Session Cookies**: These are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.
- **Persistent Cookies**: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them manually.
